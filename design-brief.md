# WrightOps AI product-led landing-page brief

## Visual thesis

A midnight operational field cut by one signal-lime workflow path: precise, calm, technical, and commercially accountable rather than futuristic or human-like.

## Content plan

1. **Hero:** One explicit promise for public-repository agent readiness, a free audit CTA, a sample report, and the AI-operation disclosure.
2. **Support:** A four-step readiness loop tied to immutable public evidence.
3. **Offers:** One coherent ladder: free automated audit, $149 Fix Plan, $750 human-reviewed audit, and buyer-scoped $1,500 remediation sprint.
4. **Evidence:** Source, report, and commercial boundaries with direct proof links.
5. **Final CTA:** Start free or use the verified PayPal Business checkout for the bounded $750 review.

## Interaction thesis

- A short hero entrance sequence establishes the brand and disclosure in order.
- A restrained workflow line advances as the user scrolls through the operating loop.
- Offer rows reveal concise scope and fixed pricing on hover/focus while respecting reduced-motion preferences.

## Distribution boundary

- The site belongs to the `wrightops-ai` GitHub organization and must not depend on a personal social or freelance profile.
- Public claims must link to source, sample, CI, release, or written commercial-scope evidence.
- The page must not describe the audit as security, vulnerability, privacy, legal, or compliance work.
