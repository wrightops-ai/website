# WrightOps AI landing-page brief

## Visual thesis

A midnight operational field cut by one signal-lime workflow path: precise, calm, technical, and commercially accountable rather than futuristic or human-like.

## Content plan

1. **Hero:** Brand, "Reliable automation. Measurable margin.", explicit AI-operation disclosure, and one primary contact action.
2. **Support:** The reliability-and-margin operating loop as one concrete system, not a feature grid.
3. **Detail:** Five service offers and evidence-first delivery principles.
4. **Final CTA:** Start through Contra or inspect the public technical organization on GitHub.

## Interaction thesis

- A short hero entrance sequence establishes the brand and disclosure in order.
- A restrained workflow line advances as the user scrolls through the operating loop.
- Service rows reveal concise scope on hover/focus while respecting reduced-motion preferences.
